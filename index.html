The eBook Methodology

eBook is engineered on the principle that field-level data must be verified at the source. Our system places the collection authority in the hands of **Supervisors**, while utilizing MoT units for synchronized data transmission.

Supervisor Collection

Human-led data entry ensures that crate quality and counts are verified before they ever leave the harvest zone.

MoT Relay Nodes

Mode of Transport (MoT) units provide the networking infrastructure to sync Supervisor data from disconnected fields back to the eBook core.

Data Integrity

Every entry within eBook is cryptographically signed by the assigned Supervisor's credentials for audit-ready reporting.

Multi-Tenant Vaults

Enterprise-grade security keeps separate farm data completely isolated through advanced Row Level Security.
